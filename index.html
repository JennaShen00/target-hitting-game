Welcome! This experiment consists of 40 trials in total.

Before we begin, you will complete 4 practice trials to get familiar with the task. On each round, press and hold the center button and keep your eyes focused on it until the targets appear. Your goal is to hit the center of one target as accurately as possible.

After each click, you will see feedback showing how far your click was from the center.

Please complete this task on a computer using a web browser.
